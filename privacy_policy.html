Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Savor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Meal Photos

We collect photos of meals that you choose to take using our App. These photos are:

  • Stored securely in Firebase Storage (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Sent to Google's Gemini API for AI-powered nutrition analysis to estimate calories and nutrition information
  • Associated with your user account and linked to your meal records
  • Used to provide calorie estimates, nutrition breakdowns, and meal tracking features

You can choose not to take photos, and the App will function normally without photo analysis features.

2.2 Nutrition Data

We collect the following nutrition-related information:

  • Calorie estimates generated by AI analysis or entered manually
  • Nutrition breakdowns including protein, carbohydrates, fats, fiber, and sugar
  • Food items and their amounts
  • Meal history, duration, and preferences
  • Hunger and satiety ratings

This data is stored in Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud Platform) and is associated with your user account.

2.3 Account Information

We collect the following account-related information:

  • Email address (for authentication and account management)
  • Meal tracking data (meal duration, meal types, timestamps)
  • Background images (if you choose to upload them for personalization)
  • Notification preferences and reminders

2.4 Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • App features you access
  • Time spent using the App
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-powered nutrition analysis of your meal photos
  • Track and display your meal history and eating habits
  • Generate analytics and insights about your meals
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Provide technical support and maintenance
  • Send you notifications and reminders (if enabled)
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may have access to your information:

4.1 Google Firebase

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Data storage (photos and nutrition data stored on Google Cloud Platform)
  • User authentication
  • Cloud Firestore database
  • Firebase Storage for photo storage

Your data is stored on Google's servers and is subject to Google's privacy practices. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Google Gemini API

We use Google's Gemini API to analyze meal photos and estimate nutrition information. When you take a photo of your meal:

  • The photo is sent to Google's servers for AI analysis
  • Google's AI processes the image to identify food items and estimate calories and nutrition
  • The analysis results are returned to our App and stored in your account

Photos sent to Google Gemini API are processed in accordance with Google's privacy practices. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Who Can Access Your Data

  • You: You can view, edit, and delete your own data at any time through the App
  • App Administrators: Authorized administrators may access your data for technical support, debugging, maintenance, and to ensure the proper functioning of the App
  • Google: As our service provider, Google has access to data stored on their servers (Firebase, Gemini API) in accordance with their privacy policy and terms of service

5.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data is encrypted at rest on Google Cloud Platform
  • Access is restricted to authenticated users only
  • User data is isolated per account with proper access controls
  • Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access your personal data through the App at any time
  • Correction: You can edit and correct your meal data, food items, and nutrition information
  • Deletion: You can delete individual meal records or your entire account and all associated data at any time
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of photo analysis by choosing not to take photos. The App functions normally without photo features
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account through the App settings, which will permanently delete all your data

To exercise these rights, you can use the App's built-in features or contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account:

  • All your meal records, photos, and nutrition data will be permanently deleted
  • Deletion is typically completed within 30 days
  • Some information may be retained in backup systems for a limited time for disaster recovery purposes

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.
